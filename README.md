 Research Focus & Focus Lanes

My research sits at the intersection of applied microeconomics, labor policy, and human capital development. I specialize in evaluating institutional frictions, qualification recognition processes, and skilled labor integration across international systems.

Lane 1: Education Economics & Returns to Non-Traditional and Digital Credentials
Lane 2: Labor Market Policy, Skilled Migration Integration & Administrative Barriers
Lane 3: Applied Microeconometrics & Public Program Evaluation (Logframe, baseline monitoring, and audit compliance)
Lane 4: Health Economics, Workplace Well-being & Operational Safety (HACCP safety parameter modeling)


 Econometric Methods & Quantitative Toolkit

Causal Inference: Difference-in-Differences (DiD), Propensity Score Matching (PSM), Instrumental Variables (IV), Regression Discontinuity Designs (RDD)
Programming Languages: Stata, R, Python, SPSS
Machine Learning & NLP: Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) Topic Modeling, Logistic and Non-Linear Regression, Automated Decision Trees


 Featured Research & Coding Repositories

[GIZ-NiCOP-Impact-Evaluation](https://github.com/princess-oledibe-econ/GIZ-NiCOP-Impact-Evaluation)
Language: Stata (`.do`) & R (`.R`)
Description: An econometric evaluation pipeline estimating the causal impact of GIZ-NiCOP agricultural trade capacity training on the export volumes of Nigerian agribusinesses. Implements Propensity Score Matching (PSM) and Difference-in-Differences (DiD) models.

[Pathogen-Holding-Regression](https://github.com/princess-oledibe-econ/Pathogen-Holding-Regression)
Language: R (`.R`)
Description: Programmatic estimation of decimal reduction times (D-values) using non-linear regressions and thermal decay curves, adapting safety standards from the New Zealand Ministry for Primary Industries (MPI) Technical Paper No: 2016/06.

[Korean-Graduate-Retention-NLP](https://github.com/princess-oledibe-econ/Korean-Graduate-Retention-NLP)
Language: Python (`.py`) & R (`.R`)
Description: Mixed-methods text-mining and statistical code evaluating immigration policy topics (via LDA) and estimating logistic regressions to determine the factors causing skilled graduate attrition in international educational systems.

[HACCP-CCP-Decision-Tree](https://github.com/princess-oledibe-econ/HACCP-CCP-Decision-Tree)
Language: Python (`.py`)
Description: An object-oriented decision-support tool automating the categorization of Critical Control Points (CCPs) in global food safety pipelines using standard CODEX Alimentarius rules.
